Oslo Kaffebar

Berlin based roastery

Oslo Kaffebar is a specialty coffee roastery

Ever since we opened in July 2012 we have been busy making sure that we turn Oslo Kaffebar into a simple, comfortable space with an emphasis on some of the best home roasted espresso and filter coffees, home baked cookies/cakes and vintage vinyl you can enjoy in Berlin. Located at Eichendorffstrasse 13, near Nordbahnhof and the convenient S1, on a picturesque side street opposite a gloriously green park, Oslo Kaffebar provides a haven for anyone looking for an extremely well prepared shot of coffee before work, after lunch or any time at all. Oslo Kaffebar mixes Berlin’s concrete, raw interiors and exteriors with a Norwegian wooden weekend house. The mix is cool, natural and relaxed and we’re sure that you’ll love spending time relaxing at Oslo Kaffebar. Follow us to know better about us and our vision here.
